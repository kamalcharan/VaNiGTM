Schema Markup
Structured data for AI retrieval

Schema markup helps AI systems understand and surface your content accurately. Toggle which schemas to auto-generate across your content.

FAQ Schema
Question-answer pairs that AI systems can extract directly. Essential for feature and pricing queries.
@type: "FAQPage" mainEntity: [{ @type: "Question", name: "What is...?", acceptedAnswer: {...} }]
HowTo Schema
Step-by-step instructions for process-oriented queries. Great for "how to automate..." content.
@type: "HowTo" name: "How to..." step: [{ @type: "HowToStep", text: "Step 1..." }]
SoftwareApp
Product details that help AI understand ContractNest's features, pricing, and category.
@type: "SoftwareApplication" name: "ContractNest" category: "BusinessApplication" offers: { price: "..." }
